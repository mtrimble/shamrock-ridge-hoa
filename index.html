Good to Know

Management Certificate on File

The Shamrock Ridge HOA management certificate is filed with the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) as required by Texas law. View it on the Documents page.

ARC Approval Required Before Projects

All exterior modifications — including fences, sheds, additions, paint color changes, and landscaping — require written approval from the Architectural Review Committee before work begins.
